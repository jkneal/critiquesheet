About CritiqueSheet.com

Get quick feedback on your performance from experienced musicians

Get feedback from hundreds of musicians, including performers, educators, and possibly one of your idols. As a student, get feedback on your playing that ranges from a few tips, to a full detailed critique. Use this information to guide your practice, then when you are ready submit another critique request to see how much you have improved, or submit to a different critiquer to gain their unique insight

Connect With Other Musicians

Master your skill by connecting with other masters

In the past, few have had the privilege and opportunity to learn from many masters. But today, thanks to technology, geographical boundaries are no longer a barrier. CritiqueSheet.com uses technology to build a community and help students connect with educators and performers all around the world!

Develop Your Skills

Reach the next level, faster, with CritiqueSheet.com

Through the process of critiquing, analysis with sophisticated tools, and competition, CritiqueSheet.com provides you a great amount of information about your current playing abilities. Use this information to implement highly effective practice sessions. Then verify you have accomplished your improvement objectives.

Promote Yourself

Build a portfolio of your work and start opening doors

As you develop and become a proficient player, your performance portfolio will keep a record of your accomplishments. Showcase your best performances and awards to attract potential job opportunities. In addition, participate in competitions on CritiqueSheet.com to win cash prizes.

Earn Money

Become a reviewer, teach students or refer others to start earning money

If you are currently a performer, educator, or proficient player, earn money on CritiqueSheet.com by helping others. Perform critiques for a fee that works for you. Or develop a study program and take on students. Finally, help build the CritiqueSheet.com community through our referral program. You keep a percentage of revenues for each member you bring in.
